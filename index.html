Japanese Soufflé Pancakes

A pancake made using soufflé techniques, where egg whites are whipped up with sugar into a glossy thick meringue then mixed with a batter made with the yolks.

Ingredients:

  • 3 Egg-yolks
  • 3 1/2tbsp milk
  • 3tbsp All-purpose flour
  • 3 Egg-whites
  • 2tbsp Granulated sugar
  • Toppings-Berries, honey, butter, etc. as desired

Procedure:

  • Step 1- To the egg-yolks, add the milk and all-purpose flour and mix thoroughly to obtain a thick paste. Set aside
  • Step 2- Whip the egg whites with an electric mixer while gradually adding the sugar. Beat until you obtain stiff peaks
  • Step 3- Add the meringue to the egg-yolk mixture in halves and fold in very gently(do NOT overmix)
  • Step 4- Gently scoop about 1 cup of the batter per pancake and place it onto the pan.
  • Step 5- Pour about 2-3tsp of water in the pan(away from the pancakes) and cover
  • Step 6- Cook for 3-4 minutes and flip
  • Step 7- Pour 2-3tsp of water, cover, and cook for another 3-4 minutes
  • Step 8- Stack onto a plate and top with berries, butter, or honey, as per your choice

Enjoy~
